Gaming PC

Custom Mid-Level Gaming PC Build

This build was done by myself and project partner, Omar Andrew Veliz, as a
personal project and learning experience.

Visuals

Specifications

CPU: AMD Ryzen 5 3600 3.6GHz 6-Core Processor

Motherboard: Gigabyte B550M AORUS PRO-P Micro ATX AM4 Motherboard

Memory: Team T-Dorce Delta RGB 16GB (2 x 8GB) DDR4-3600 CL 18 Memory

Storage: SK Hynix Gold P31 1TB M.2-2280 NVME Solid State Drive

Video Card: MSI GeForce RTX 3060 12GB Gaming X Video card

Case: darkFlash DLM 21 Micro ATX Mid Tower Case

Power Supply:Corsair RM (2019) 750W 80+ Gold Certified Fully Modular ATX Power Supply

Operating System: Windows 10

This project took some time to complete due to the shortage of micro chips in todays world. This makes it pretty difficult to obtain a video card for more of a quality display. During the duration of this project a lot was learned about the physical architecture of a PC. The PC currently runs on a Windows 10 operating system and can compete with some of the leading gaming consoles.

The PC is a mid-level design however one could not argue against the fact that it is very aesthetically pleasing, especially in a dark room. There are currently six cooling fans in the PC, five independent RGB fans and one modular CPU cooling fan, for premium cooling and efficiency.
